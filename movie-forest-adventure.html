Synopsis

In a magical forest where every creature glows with inner light, four unlikely friends embark on an epic adventure to save their home from darkness. When an ancient shadow threatens to extinguish the Lumina Woods forever, brave fox Finn, clever rabbit Ruby, wise owl Oliver, and playful squirrel Sammy must journey to the Heart of the Forest.

Along the way, they discover that true friendship and believing in yourself are the most powerful magic of all. A heartwarming animated adventure for the whole family!
