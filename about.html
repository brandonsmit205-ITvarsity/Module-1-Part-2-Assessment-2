Who We Are

Pine City Mall is a premier shopping and entertainment destination located in the heart of the city. Since opening our doors in 2010, we have been committed to providing an exceptional shopping experience for our visitors.

With over 100 stores, a state-of-the-art cinema complex, diverse dining options, and world-class amenities, Pine City Mall is more than just a shopping center - it's a lifestyle destination.
